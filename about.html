Hello, I'm Cat

I am a frontend developer + designer with a background in event design and architecture.

Armed with 15+ years of experience designing physical spaces, I apply a systems-thinking approach to solving complex design problems.

While designing corporate events for TechCrunch, Tableau, and SaaStr, I gained exposure to innovative software products and was intrigued by the realm of opportunities available in software engineering. This exposure led me to exploring UX/UI design and eventually joining a full stack web development training course with Southern Methodist University where my interest in front end development blossomed.

I champion collaborating with engineers of all levels in the design process to create efficient + creative solutions and am eager to work with designers to create products that consumers love.

Hi! My name is Cat Jalomo and I am a Front-end developer + designer based in Dallas, Texas. I have a background in Architecture + Interior Design and worked in the commercial architecture & interior design industries for 10 years where I worked on renovations to the San Antonio River Walk, new construction fire stations and museums, and renovations/new construction on the Texas border, as well as military air fields and bases.
I then transitioned into a Vegas-based production company where I was lucky enough to work with B2B corporate events. I have 4+ years of experience in exhibit design + live events, UX/UI design, and gained valuable experience as lead design contact for high-profile clients. Even more valuable, was witnessing happy clients' reactions when a project was delivered on time, under budget, with flawless execution (and when the environments looked exactly like my 3D renderings!).
